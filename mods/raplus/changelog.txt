General changes
Eliminated individual Nation subfactions, unifying them into Allies and Soviet
Prequisites for units reset on owner change. Its still possible to build other faction arsenal by capturing their Construction Yard
All Artillery units, Sniper, Mortar Trooper Cruisers, Missile Submarine reveal their position for an brief duration when firing their weaponry
Giants Ants as map creeps made possible, added checkbox for it(disabled by Default). When enabled mineshafts will periodically spawn Giant Ants that are hostile to all.
Made Giant Ants use predefined colors
Added three new types of Giant Ant: Tesla Ant(blue) that attacks Tesla Tank weaponry, Toxic Ant(green) that sprays Chemicals at close range, Shadow Ant(black) that is invisible.
Advice intervall for Low Power and Silos Needed audio increased to every 5 min
Units armed with Tesla weaponry uses power now, -2 Power for each Shock Trooper and -4 for each Tesla Tank and Tesla Boat
Only Veteran units can eject an Pilot, the Pilot can enter any friendly vehicle or aircraft for promoting it, increasing recipient's rank by rank of himself. Air units have more probability of spawning an survivor than ground vehicles, the chance decreases as the rank increases.
Chronosphere duration increased to 30s for regular version, advanced stays at 20s

Mapping changes
Maps are properly categorized now (1v1, 2v2, 3v3, FFA, Naval, Fun, Mini, etc.)
There is an amazing collection of new maps included with the mod
Defined clones of existing theatres for porting TD maps to RA and did so
Added 3 new tilesets: Jungle, Tropic and Underground


Economical changes
Major Economy change: Ore Refinery discards excess resources now. Do actually manage your economy!
Silos provide secondary income now, aslong they are powered.
Mine and Gem Mine footprint edited to become ground passable, that means it will no longer block any ground unit's movement.
Added an extra type of Mine, the Ore Drill mine generates Ore twice as fast, its impassable unlike the shafts.

Building changes
Defense buildings can be built at 3c away from base structures, instead of 2c

All Civilian and Tech buildings spawn an husk now, like they do in TD. Restored the distinct names of civilian buildings from TD.
The charging process of ANY Support Power pauses now when Low on Power
