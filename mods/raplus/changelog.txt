General changes
Eliminated individual Nation subfactions, unifying them into Allies and Soviet
Prequisites for units reset on owner change. Its still possible to build other faction arsenal by capturing their Construction Yard
Submarines have their own prequisite class, seperate of Ships. They also have their own ^Submarine default unit
All defenses Stealth detection gets automatically paused when on Low power
All Artillery units, Allies Cruiser and Soviet Missile Submarine reveal their position for an brief duration when firing their weaponry. Note: Missile Sub doesnt reveal position when engaging Air units using its AA weaponry
Naval overhaul: Both factions have Ships and Submarines. Soviets have slightly different counterparts
Major Economy change: Ore Refinery discards excess resources now. Do actually manage your economy!
Maps are properly categorized now (1v1, 2v2, 3v3, FFA, Naval, Fun, Mini, etc.)
There is an amazing collection of new maps included with the mod
Defined clones of existing theatres for porting TD maps to RA and did so
Giants Ants as map creeps made possible, added checkbox for it(disabled by Default). When enabled mineshafts will periodically spawn Giant Ants that are hostile to all.
Mine and Gem Mine footprint edited to become ground passable, that means it will no longer block any ground unit's movement.

Graphical changes
Medium, Heavy and Mammoth Tanks got an new muzzle fx for their main gun cannon attacks
All building Make anims have Tick: 80
Added death anims with Tick: 800 for most base buildings
Added eyecandy smoke to Power Plant, Ore Refinery, Advanced Power Plant and Bio Lab
Added Building husks for purely decorative usage during mapmaking

Building changes
Defense buildings can be built at 3c away from base structures, instead of 2c
Defense buildings get their cloak detection paused when on low power. This does not affect the Pillboxes
Ship Yard and Sub Pen building requirement radius reduced in half to 4c, down to 4c from 8c
Fake structures have their own queue, that does not display an progress bar above the Construction Yard
Fake structures are built consistent at 1/4 of original price, instead 10% of original. The prices are rounded down where applicable(Fake FIX 185 instead 187)
All Civilian and Tech buildings spawn an husk now, like they do in TD. Restored the distinct names of civilian buildings from TD.
